    Producing Legacy Products

    GHI Electronic's products are all engineered and manufactured in-house. While some of our older products are deemed legacy and not recommended for new designs, we can still produce them, however a minimum order may be required.

    We understand the need for continued product support and availability. Please contact us to discuss ways to fulfill your immediate needs and also to discuss upgrade options.

    Visit our main website at www.ghielectronics.com and our community forums at forums.ghielectronics.com.
